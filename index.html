WELCOME TO SANKHLA CORPORATE SERVICES PVT. LTD.

Sankhla Consultant

M.S. Sankhla & Co. was founded in 2002. In 2011, Sarshan Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. was established as a business entity. In 2018, the company rebranded to Sarshan HR Solutions India Private Limited, focusing on providing outsourced labor law consultancy services to corporate clients, as well as comprehensive labor and industrial law compliance services. We have been active in the fields of payroll management and labour law compliance for several years.
